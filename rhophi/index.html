Trust Structure

General Assets

General Assets flow from the founder (ρφ) into the central pool, managed by the main RhoPhi Trust. These assets generate dividends distributed to Founders as primary beneficiaries.

General asset dividend rate to Founders.

Holdings Assets

Holdings Assets are managed by the main RhoPhi Trust and comprise corporate subsidiaries and investment vehicles. Capital flows into portfolio companies and returns as distributions.


Customer Assets are managed by individual customer trusts, with each customer as sole beneficiary holding the right to determine their own trust deed.

LAYERED TRUST ARCHITECTURE

Customer Trust Model

For each customer (e.g. Customer "A"):


Ownership — RhoPhi Trust
Management — "A" Trust
Beneficiary — Customer "A"


Customer "A" assets are a pro-rata beneficiary of General Assets of RhoPhi, with right to determine the trust deed for their own trust.

PROPRIETARY CUSTOMER BENEFICIARY

Dual Jurisdictions

RhoPhi Holdings operates under a dual-jurisdiction structure spanning the United Kingdom and the Republic of Ghana — enabling capital deployment, asset protection, and trust law benefits across both regulatory environments.
